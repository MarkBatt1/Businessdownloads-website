INSTRUCTIONS FOR AI:

Please generate a complete grant application that includes:

1. Executive Summary (150-200 words)
   - Brief overview of business and funding request
   - Key impact statistics

2. Business Background (200-250 words)
   - Company history and structure
   - Current operations and market position
   - Track record and achievements

3. Project Description (250-300 words)
   - Detailed explanation of what funding will be used for
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Specific deliverables

4. Impact & Benefits (200-250 words)
   - Economic impact (jobs, growth, turnover)
   - Community benefits
   - Long-term sustainability
   - Measurable outcomes

5. Financial Justification (150-200 words)
   - Budget breakdown
   - Value for money
   - Financial viability
   - Return on investment for funder

6. Risk Management (100-150 words)
   - Potential challenges and mitigation strategies
   - Contingency plans

7. Conclusion (100 words)
   - Summary of why funding should be awarded
   - Call to action for grant assessor

- Use active voice
- Include specific numbers and metrics where possible
- Address grant criteria directly
- Show understanding of funder's priorities

Tips for Better AI Results
